Whether you are searching for something unique, looking for that perfect gift, hunting for a bargain or just browsing, the wandrshop community is here to help. Wherever you go, we want you to enjoy shopping while travelling. Here are some of the current features:

  • MY TRIP

    Plan ahead and save shops, tips, photos and topics onto an itinerary so you can fit in that amazing shopping trip while you enjoy the sites

  • EXPLORE

    Discover more about different shops, brands and destinations on this feature page

  • SEARCH

    Search for recommended shops in the world's greatest shopping destinations. Pick a city, pick a category…that’s it!

  • GROUP

    Learn about shopping destinations from your fellow wandrshoppers – where they recommend and why

  • ME

    Create a personal page for easy access to your favourite shops, brands and destinations

  • SHOPPING TOOLS

    Access handy tools like sizing guides, tax free shopping and common shopping phrases like how to spot a sale in any language
